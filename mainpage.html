Art

3.29.16


Going to a Ririe-Woodbury performance on campus today was a keen idea.

There was a piece called "50 Years" that was loosely based on how Utah wasn't given statehood for 50 years.

There was a lot of odd chanting in it and no music. It looked like disheveled nomads entertaining themselves and attempting to make themselves comfortable for fifty years.

Its oddity was the talk of the people leaving the theater.

Somehow it smoothed out bumps in my heart.

I feel a lot better.

It was worth it, even though I finished my Chinese Remainder Theorem project for Discrete Math at one.
